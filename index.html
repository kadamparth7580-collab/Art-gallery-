A Letter, Left Here On Purpose
If you've reached this room, you've already seen every photograph. But that's the funny thing. None of these were the reason I built this place. Photographs are easy. They capture what someone looked like. What I've always been trying to hold onto is what it feels like when you're around. The way ordinary days become memories. The way a random moment becomes important without asking for permission. The way someone slowly becomes part of your life until you can't remember what it felt like before. So this gallery was never about twenty pictures. It was about one person. And if I'm being honest, even twenty rooms wouldn't be enough. Because no frame has ever managed to capture my favorite thing about you. It's not your smile. Not your eyes. Not any photograph hanging on these walls. It's you. Thank you for every memory that arrived disguised as an ordinary day. And thank you for being my favorite story that never needed pages. Love you. ❤️
— Always
